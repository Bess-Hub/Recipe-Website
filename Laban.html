Labne

Ingredients

1L of whole milk

2tbsp of kefir

Step-by-Step

1. Put the milk in a high pot and take it to the stove at low fire.
2. Once the milk is about to boil turn off the stove and let the milk cool down to 120F.



3. Once the milk is at the right temperature add the 2 table spoons of kefir and stir for 30 seconds.
4. Close the pot with a lid, put a blanket on top of it and let it rest for 8 hours.
5. After 8 hours, uncover the pot, remove the lid, the content should look like a regular yogurt with a bit of water on top.
6. Place the content in a coffee filter or a cloth and let it drain the excess of liquid. I usually leave it for 10 hours.
7. Place the content in a glass container, add salt and mix.
8. To serve you can put olive oil, and season it. Serve with bread.
Enjoy this great entrance!
